FIVE TIPS TO FOCUS ON YOUR STUDY.

1) Keep Your phones and tablets away from your study room: As we have already discussed before, it is not a good idea to study while chatting with your friends in between. Now, that's another case when you have to study online as it is a must to do, but whenever you are free, try to read books. And if you want to use your device then use for a good purpose. You should chat only when you are chatting. You should study only when your are studying. And if you want to study on your device. Then visit knowledgeable and safe websites to gain more knowledge and be enlightened.

So, back to topic, we know distraction is the worst enemy when you are studying. Distraction can be anything. Like your phone, your food, a noise etc. So, always make sure to study when you are in these particular conditions where there is no distraction.

a) No loud noise.

b) No phones or any other device in your room.

c) Don't eat whenever you are studying. This can cause a major distraction because while eating, your brain relishes the meal and almost forgets what you were doing. Now, this is not the case everytime. You may eat, but whenever you are going for an exam or something, make sure to follow this tip.

*Also, if you want your device to be with you, then just turn off the notifications and study calmly.


2) Study in a peaceful environment: It is often seen that students need peace to study which is very difficult for them to manage in areas which are prone to high pitched sound. But you can use your ear cups if this is the case! And for those who have a peaceful nature, they should close their room, worship your gods and they study peacefully. Make sure there should be least disturbance in your study and focus. Eat almonds and drink a lot of milk to study even better. And remember, study not just for your exams but study for your whole life!


3) Study in good light: Now this point just states that you should always study in a good light. That is, the light that you are studying in should neither be too dim, nor be too bright. Nowadays, kids are always busy on their phones and see them all day till night when they don't even know how harmful is the effect of blue light on their eyes. Blue light actually harms your eyes when you are seeing your device. But, there is an advanced option to save your eyes from the harmful blue rays. You may just turn on the night light button and then study! Useful.

Kids have eye issues mainly due to this. So you should must follow this tip.


4) Set your time and learn according to it: Set a good timetable and work according to it. It is the best way to organize yourself.


5) Avoid Distractions And Don't Study When you are hungry: Study in a peaceful environment and study with full concentration. Also, when you are hungry, then do not study. Study only when you are satisfied and fulfilled. Be Nourished!


I hope you'll follow these tips and focus on your education and avoid distraction.
