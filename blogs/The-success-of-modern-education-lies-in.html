The Success Of Modern Education Lies In Inculcating Moral Education

What we've learnt from our childhood, the moral values and behavioral attitude is the thing that is making us stick to our roots even today. The modern education recently has been claimed to be a nuisance by the students. The moral education that lived in the minds of the young, is now getting destroyed so badly that schools aren't schools anymore. Now, who's to blame? Who's to blame? A very bad habit of us students is that we do the crime and although knowing we are the culprit, we allegedly blame someone else. We have started thinking ourselves too grown up. So much that we can't even tolerate the lectures of the teacher who is actually trying to make us realize our own mistakes. The modern students have fallen down so much that they have lost the ability to comprehend all the moral education that they had learnt so lovingly back in class 1.


After the reopening of the schools, what was expected was something great, but what became the reality is beyond the expressions. They've forgotten their roots. But, if we think about it deeply. we'll get to know that it's not really their fault. The social media monster has changed the shape of the brains of the youngsters during the online era in the COVID breakout.


Now, when we talk about the success of modern education, then things will get better only when we students try to do so. We students need to understand what is right and what is wrong.


Moral education makes life successful. It helps to eliminate problems like, violence, dishonesty, jealousy etc. from one's life. It can wipe out bad influences in the society like child abuse and disregarding women. It also helps in building a positive approach to things, self confidence and motivation.


From a young age, the children are able to get exposure to different disciplines and also being able to connect to its moral aspect. Thinking - Moral education also helps in developing critical thinking among the students as they tend to become more rational and understanding of the situation around them.


So basically, if today's generation inculcates the moral values and applies them to real life, then learning will become so much enjoyable with everyone! Then, the modern education will become a success, because school is a place where students not only come to study, but to develop themselves into a capable and a better human. And these moral values would make a mind get enlightened. Because of which the education that they'll be getting can be comprehended and implemented in the best possible way!
